Indie bestselling comic artist & Author

Rose Bousamra

Rose Bousamra is an indie bestselling comic artist and author inspired by nature, fantasy worlds and alternate realities. They have two graphic novels with First Second Books, including Frizzy (2022) with author Claribel Ortega, and solo debut graphic novel Gutless (2025).

They have also done work for IDW, Power & Magic Press, and CollegeHumor/DropoutTV. When not reading or creating comics, they can be found playing video games, wrangling their tiny dog and two cats, or wandering the woods around Detroit, Michigan.
