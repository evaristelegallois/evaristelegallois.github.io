Rose Bousamra

Rose Bousamra is an indie bestselling comic artist and author inspired by nature, fantasy worlds and alternate realities. They have two graphic novels with First Second Books, including Frizzy (2022) with author Claribel Ortega, and solo debut graphic novel Gutless (2025).
